Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does it take to receive a timepiece?

In-stock timepieces ship within 2-3 business days. Bespoke commissions require 6-9 months as each is handcrafted to order by a single master watchmaker.

Do you accept trade-ins or consignments?

Yes. We accept luxury timepieces from major houses in trade or consignment. Contact our pre-owned department for valuation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept wire transfer, major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), cryptocurrency, and financing through our partner institutions.

Can I visit the manufacture in Geneva?

Private atelier tours are available by appointment for clients and serious collectors. Tours are limited to maintain our craftspeople's focus.

How often should I service my Aurum?

We recommend comprehensive servicing every 5-7 years. However, if your watch keeps excellent time and shows no issues, it can run longer between services.
